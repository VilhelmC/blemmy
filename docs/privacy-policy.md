# Privacy Policy (Draft Source)

## Who this applies to

This policy applies to users who sign in and store CV documents in the cloud
features of this app.

## What we collect

- Authentication data provided by your sign-in provider.
- CV content you create and save.
- Document metadata (names, timestamps, version history).
- Share-link metadata and share access outcomes.
- Privacy consent and request records.

## Why we process data

- To provide cloud save, version history, and share-link features.
- To protect service security and detect abuse.
- To fulfill legal obligations for privacy requests.

## Legal bases

- Contract necessity for core cloud features.
- Legitimate interests for service security and operational integrity.
- Legal obligation for handling privacy rights.

## Share links

- Share links are token-based, revocable, and can expire.
- Links only grant read-only access to one shared document.

## Data retention

- You can request deletion of your cloud data.
- Share logs and privacy request records are retained for limited audit and
  security periods.
- Detailed retention periods are listed in internal retention controls and
  reflected in app behavior.

## Your rights

- Request access/export of your personal data.
- Request deletion of your personal data.
- Review and manage your consent/notice state where presented in the app.

## Contact

For privacy requests, use the in-app privacy controls first. If unavailable,
contact the operator via the configured support channel.
